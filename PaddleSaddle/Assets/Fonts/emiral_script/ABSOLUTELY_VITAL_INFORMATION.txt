Emiral Script is a vintage typeface in Regular and Bold. 
The font is created by Måns Grebäck and works great for logotypes and slogans. 
It has contextual alternates and ligatures, and supports hundreds of languages. 

Write an underscore after any word to make a swash.
Example: Emiral_

If your software supports ligatures, you can write multiple underscores for a longer line. 
Example: Oldtimer__
